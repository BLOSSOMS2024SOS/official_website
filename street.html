STREET THEATRE


Rules


  • Duration: 8 + 2 minutes (including performance area entry and exit)
  • Language: English Thematic (the dialogues, narration, etc of the play should be told in English)
  • No of participants: Maximum of 25. Teams are NOT allowed to change the participants for the finals.
  • Jingle can be in any language (Every team has to have a minimum of 4 language in singing jingles)
  • Usage of loudspeakers will NOT be permitted.
  • 10ft is the maximum permitted height for any formations during the street play. No girls and boys lifts allowed.
  • Slot numbers to be provided by the deanery.
  • Teams/Productions in each deanery are expected to perform only one play as the production.
  • Script of each play needs to be submitted at least 7 days prior to the event date to SWO coordinator and deanery event coordinator for vetting.
  • Each deanery should have a minimum of 5 teams to compete in the prelims from which 2 teams will be selected for the finals. Deaneries having fewer than 5 teams in Prelims, only 1 team will be selected to go to finals.

NOTE: Vulgarity in movements and costume will NOT be entertained. Usage of fire and water on stage is NOT allowed.
