Inter Deanery Dance Extravaganza


Rules


  • Event will be held on the days of IN-BLOOM and BLOSSOMS on 15 and 16 March 2024.
  • Every team Participating will be given grades and points (Based on the performance).
  • First, second and third place will be given A, B and C respectively and every team participating from each deanery will be graded either D or E based on their performances. This may ensure every Team participating will add to the overall points of the Deanery.
  • Minimum number of teams from a deanery : 4
  • Maximum Number of teams per deanery: 8
  • Minimum number of dancers in a team: 25 and no maximum limit
  • Minimum time limit per team: 7 minutes
  • Maximum Time Limit per team: 8 minutes
  • Maximum number of songs per team: 3
  • Maximum number of members in a team and maximum audience support for the event are the most important criteria for grading.
  • Instruments, props, and costume usage are optional, and concentration is given to the dance to be performed by the deanery or team. The team needs to bear expenses of any costumes used, and the deanery does not take charge of the same.
  • Songs should be from Indian movies or folk songs.
  • The registration details, which include the names and registration numbers of the team members and the selected song, must be submitted to the Deanery Coordinators on or before March 5, 2024, by the team leader.
  • The teacher coordinator is responsible for ensuring that songs are not repeated within the deanery.
  • If there is repetition of songs between any two teams between other deaneries, the team that comes first with the song will be given preference, and the other team will be asked to change their song.
  • The teacher coordinators have to make sure that the songs chosen from each department have songs from all Indian languages, especially Kannada, Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, etc., and not only Hindi.
  • Slots will be picked by the teacher coordinator on March 12th, 2024.
  • Deanery Blossoms coordinators should make sure that their respective Deanery students are present during the event and encourage their teams performances for their sessions.
